Medical Billing Process

How Does Our Medical Billing Process Work?

We offer a 98% success rate because of the medical billing process we follow. Our billing process paves the path for improved practice revenue.

1

Patient Registration

We Begin By Collecting Patient Details. Accurate Demographic Data Helps Prevent Claim Denials And Ensures Smooth Billing Later In The Process.

2

Charge Entry

We Enter The Service Charges And Determine The Eligible Reimbursement Amount For Each Procedure Or Treatment Provided.

3

Claim Submission

The Prepared Claim Is Submitted To The Insurance Company For Processing And Payment.

4

Claim Evaluation

The Insurance Provider Evaluates The Submitted Claim To Verify Accuracy, Compliance, And Eligibility.

5

Patient Billing

Once Insurance Payments Are Received, The Remaining Balance is Calculated And Billed To The Patient.

6

Patient Statement

A Detailed Statement is Shared With The Patient, Showing Any Remaining Balance They Need To Pay.

7

Follow-Ups

Our Billing Experts Consistently Monitor The Process And Follow Up To Ensure Timely Reimbursements And Resolve Any Issues.
